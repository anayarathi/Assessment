Food
Food items from South Asia are generally rich in flavour created through a range of herbs, spices and other ingredients. Popular foods include are samosas, biryani, and naan.

Clothing
Clothing items usually contain wrapped and draped elements. They are colorful embroidered and pattern garments. Common ones are called sarees and lehengas.

Events
Hinduism contains of multiple festivals and events. A few popular ones are Diwali, which is the festival of lights and Holi which is the festival of colours.
